Privacy Policy

At BURGUR, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed when you visit or make a purchase from our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, delivery address, payment information.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, time zone, pages visited.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser version.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill orders and deliver products.
  • To communicate with you about your order.
  • To personalize your experience on our website.
  • To improve our products, services, and website performance.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with trusted third parties only to perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing or delivery logistics, and only under strict data protection agreements.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You may also opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience and analyze usage. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the revised policy on this page.
